About Us

Welcome to Bee Blossom, your trusted partner in sustainable agriculture, located at 6973 Coach Lamp Drive, Chilliwack, British Columbia. Established as a hobby in 2014 and becoming a commercial venture in 2022, Bee Blossom has grown from a passion project into a dedicated enterprise committed to excellence and sustainability.

We specialize in premium pollination services, honey production, and nucleus colonies for beekeepers. At Bee Blossom, we understand the crucial role bees play in agriculture and ecosystem health. Our pollination services help farmers maximize crop yields and promote biodiversity, ensuring a thriving and balanced environment.

Our honey production reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability. We maintain healthy bee colonies and follow stringent standards to ensure the purity and taste of our honey. Each jar is a testament to our meticulous care, from hive to table. Our honey is available for purchase on Facebook Marketplace and through wholesale, bringing the rich flavors of Chilliwack to a broader audience.

In addition to pollination and honey production, we offer resilient and productive nucleus colonies for sale, providing a strong foundation for successful beekeeping operations.

At Bee Blossom, we are more than just beekeepers - we are stewards of the environment, advocates for agriculture, and partners in progress. Join us in our mission to support healthy ecosystems, thriving farms, and sustainable communities.
