faq

How many hive do i need per acre for apple?

For effective pollination of apple orchards, it's recommended to have one to two hives per acre. This ensures better fruit set and higher yields. Factors like hive strength and local conditions can influence the exact number needed.

How much time does it take to pollinate blueberries?

Blueberries typically require 2 to 5 weeks for effective pollination, depending on bloom duration and bee activity.

What is the best time for pollination?

The best time for pollination is during the peak bloom period of the crop, typically when most flowers are open and receptive.

What can make the pollination effective?

Effective pollination relies on factors like abundant pollen, active pollinator presence, favorable weather, healthy plants, proximity of pollinators, and timing.
